Our Story

A family kitchen that grew into Vavuniya's trusted bakery

Cake House was founded in 2012 by Thuraiyappa Loganathan and Loganathan Shyamala out of a simple love for baking. What began as cakes made for neighbours and family occasions has grown into a name people across Vavuniya trust for birthdays, weddings, and everyday treats.

Today, with 25 years of combined baking experience, every cake — from a simple butter cake to an elaborate wedding centrepiece — is still made by hand, with the same care as the very first order.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order for bulk cakes? +

Bulk orders are accepted for butter, chocolate, and veg cakes starting from 3kg. Please call or WhatsApp us a day in advance for large quantities.

How much lead time do icing cakes need? +

For birthday icing cakes, we recommend 2–3 days' notice. Wedding cakes and elaborate designs need at least 1–2 weeks so we can plan the design and tiers with you.

Do you deliver, or is it pickup only? +

Pickup is available daily from our Mannar Rd location. Delivery within Vavuniya can be arranged — just mention your address when sending an inquiry.

How do I pay? +

Payment is accepted on pickup or delivery, in cash. For large custom orders, a small advance may be requested to confirm your booking.

How is the price for icing cakes decided? +

Icing cakes are custom-quoted based on size, design complexity, and decorations. Send us your idea (and a reference photo if you have one) via the inquiry form and we'll get back to you with a price.
